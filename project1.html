Independent Engineer Services during Operation & Maintenance Period for 4 laning of Kagal-Satara Section NH-48 (Old NH-4) from Km 592.240 to Km 725.000 in the State of Maharashtra under NHDP Phase-I on BOT Basis (Length: 132 km)



Kolhapur/Satara,India
DATE: January 2019- September 2019
CLIENT: NATIONAL HIGHWAY AUTHORITY OF INDIA PIU- KOLHAPUR
FUNDING:
DURATION: 9 MONTHS

Project Overview


Call by carrying out Road conditions surveys preparing the Detailed project report during the Operation and Maintenance period using Modern Survey Technology Such as:

a) Surface defects detection and roughness measurement using Network SurveyVehicle
b) Pavement strength measurement using FWD
c) Bridge inspection using Mobile Bridge Inspection Unit
d) Road signs inspection using Retro Reflectometer
e) Traffic count using Automatic Traffic Counter cum Classifier (ATCC)


The Tasks carried out are as follows-

1. Measurement of pavement surface defects and roughness
2. Measurement of pavement Strength
3. Bridge inspection using Mobile Bridge Inspection Unit
4. Measurement of retro reflection of road signs
5. Performance Assessment of Road Markings
6. Traffic sampling and video recording
